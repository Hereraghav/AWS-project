Intro

Hello there! I'm Raghav Mittal, a final-year student at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT). Hailing from Yamunanagar, Haryana, I am a passionate and dedicated individual with a keen interest in the world of technology. During my academic journey, I have delved deep into the realms of computer science and software development. As a tech enthusiast, I have always been captivated by the seamless fusion of creativity and innovation that the tech industry offers. Beyond the realm of technology, I also have a keen interest in Finance. Staying updated with the latest financial news and market trends is something that fascinates me. I believe in the importance of understanding the financial landscape as it plays a crucial role in shaping the world of technology and business. This summer, I am excitedly diving into the world of DevOps during my training. Embracing this cutting-edge discipline, I am eager to optimize workflows, streamline processes, and enhance collaboration in software development and deployment. My journey in the tech world has been exhilarating, and I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. Apart from coding and finance, I enjoy contributing to the open-source community and staying informed about the latest trends. On this website, I aim to share my experiences, projects, and insights. From web development to DevOps and finance-related news, this platform will showcase my efforts to make a positive impact in the digital landscape. If you share the same passion for technology and finance or wish to collaborate on exciting projects, I would love to connect with you. Feel free to reach out through the contact form below. Let's embark on a journey of innovation and exploration together!
